10 Golden Rules

These are the 10 golden rules recommended for using the task manager application effectively:

  1. Don't postpone tasks that take.
  2. Create clear tasks in your agenda.
  3. Start with the hardest tasks(eat the frog).
  4. Set work sessions of 25 minutes.
  5. Take 5-minute breaks between sessions.
  6. After 4 sessions, take a longer break.
  7. Eliminate distractions.
  8. Track your progress.
  9. Be flexible and adjust as needed.
  10. Review and plan regularly.
